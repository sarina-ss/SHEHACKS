Introduction

Welcome to Budget Buddy! We know the saving and spending of money can be confusing, so we'll walk you through how to do just that. Your personal Budget Buddy will help you visualize the ins and outs of being a cost effiecent and economical consumer.

The 4 W's of Budgetting

Why is this important: managing your finances is something everyone has to eventually deal with. Budget Buddy aims to encourage your intrests in the financial world, as well as facilitate the development of these necessary budgetting skills. Unless you're constantly working with money, becoming financially literate is a lot easier said than done - so do us the honour of starting that journey here with us!

Where should I spend my money: focus on the necessities! We are not discouraging you buying your new PS5 using your saved up Christmas money - but what we are saying is be responsible! Think through your purchases, whther they are an investment in your intrests, or in a good lunch. There is a difference between needs and wants, where a need is something you cannot live without (ex. food, water, shelter) and a want can be almost anything else! Although it sounds hard to spend wisely, that's exactly what we hope to help you to do!

Who can I ask: a trusted adult, guardian or accountant. When coming to dealing with your money and how to budget it, ask someone who is reliable. Obviously, we at Budget Buddy are also here to answer all your questions, so please feel free to contact us and ask away!

When should I start: As soon as possible! It's never too early to start saving, so why not get started now? Sign Up Here
